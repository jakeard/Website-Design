Overview

Bored Co was created in October 2020 by Jake Ard. It was made as a way for people that area searching for new and exciting activities to get ideas and discounts on those activities. Our goal is to get those interested in new and exciting experiences to be able to do so at lower costs than would normally be available to them.

Business Model

We make money based on how many of our customers choose to go on a payed new experience through the links on our site. When a customer clicks the link and pays for a service from the company in the link, we gain a partial part of that sale for encouraging buying on their site. They also offer us great deals on their services for our customers, creating an easy choice for those looking for discounts. We also run a small amount of advertisements on our app.
