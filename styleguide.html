Bored Co

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to allow our audience to receive inspiration, information, and ideas. These things come from recommending and giving information on different activities to do while bored. The audience will be able to choose something interesting to them, get a list of activities that are similar to that interest, and then be given information about it and how they can do that activity. They can receive this all from the safety and comfort of their homes as they browse this website. It was created as a way to overcome the sometimes inevitable onset of boredom. It also links to different websites, where if people book or pay for something, this website gets a commission. This website is available 24/7, and anyone can receive information from anywhere.

Audience Statement

The intended audience is anybody and everybody, with an emphasis on people who are bored, do not know what to do, and want to have some fun or just learn about different activities. Anybody that does not like to have or learn about different fun and interesting activities should not use this website. Customers can come from any occupation, and can be of any age. The only requirement is that they need to be able to access the website, putting most people in almost any economic class. We are aimed towards people who are looking for new activites, but also for anyone who just wants information. Anybody can visit at any time for any reason. The variety allows the appeal to go to everyone, as it involves activities in many different areas. The audience chooses to come to this site to get ideas, find websites to book activites, or learn about new activities. The website will mainly be accessed through desktop computers and laptops, but may be accessed through mobile devices as well.
